Your VAST Endpoint

Load this URL into your CTV ad server or test player. The response XML will always reflect the latest pod configuration. Changes to the pod are saved automatically.

Values in brackets are placeholders. A VAST-compliant player will substitute [CACHEBUSTER], [CACHEBUSTING] and [DURATION] for you on each break request. Both break_id and cb take cache-buster values — a unique token per request is all the tool needs to correlate tracking pixels with a break, and cache-buster macros are more widely supported than a dedicated break-ID macro. For players that don't auto-substitute, replace them by hand with a fresh token, another fresh token, and the pod duration the player is trying to fill (in seconds). The session_id is pinned to your browser and should be left as-is.
